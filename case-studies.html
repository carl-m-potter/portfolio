1. Printer Setup UX Improvement

Problem: Users struggled with printer setup & Wi-Fi configuration, generating high support calls.

Research: Behavioural analytics, survey feedback, call driver review.

Action: Created Setup tab and self-help content journeys to guide users efficiently.

Impact: CSAT improvement from 66% → 78%, reduced avoidable support interactions.

2. Feedback & Survey Visibility Optimization

Problem: Low survey participation limited actionable insights.

Research: Behaviour tracking + survey completion analysis.

Action: Designed pop-up survey prompts to increase visibility and completion.

Impact: Higher response rate, richer qualitative insight.

3. Contact Support Journey Enhancements

Problem: Users struggled to locate self-help content efficiently.

Research: Call driver analysis and behavioural data.

Action: Redesigned journey and surfaced relevant content for faster resolution.

Impact: Reduced avoidable support interactions, improved user satisfaction.
