Survey

UX & Customer Experience Specialist

Designed and analysed a multi-market survey that increased actionable insights by 50%, driving data-led improvements to support journeys.

Challenge

User feedback was inconsistent across markets, limiting the team’s ability to make data-driven decisions. Existing surveys were poorly targeted and generated few actionable insights.

Approach

I designed a multi-market survey with clear goals, targeted questions, and structured data collection. I coordinated across regions, ensured high response rates, and analysed the data to identify patterns and opportunities for improvement.

Impact

The survey increased actionable insights by 50%, enabling teams to optimise support journeys and improve customer satisfaction. Decisions became evidence-based, and regional teams could prioritise interventions effectively.
