Setup

UX & Customer Experience Specialist

Streamlined project setup across 43 EMEA countries, reducing prep errors and accelerating launch by 30%.

Challenge

The project setup across 43 EMEA countries was inconsistent, causing delayed launches and errors in workflows. Teams struggled with misaligned goals and unclear processes.

Approach

I standardised goals, mapped workflows, and prepared tools to reduce complexity and improve alignment across teams. I created clear templates, checklists, and a shared resource library to support consistent setup.

Impact

Launch prep time decreased by 30%, errors were reduced, and teams reported clearer workflows for ongoing projects. The standardised approach improved collaboration across multiple countries and departments.
