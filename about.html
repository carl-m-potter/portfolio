My Approach to UX & CX

I approach user experience with empathy and evidence. By combining behavioural data, surveys, and user journey analysis, I identify friction points and design actionable solutions. Collaboration with product and engineering teams ensures improvements are feasible and impactful.

  • Empathy: Understand the user’s perspective to drive meaningful change.
  • Data-informed: Analyse quantitative and qualitative sources to uncover friction.
  • Insight-to-action: Recommendations tied to measurable outcomes.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams to implement improvements.
