About Me

I’m a UX & Customer Experience Specialist with nearly a decade of experience designing and optimising large-scale digital support journeys across EMEA. At Canon, I’ve led UX improvements across 43 support sites serving over 4.5 million monthly users.

My strength lies in understanding systems end-to-end. I don’t approach UX as isolated screens or interaction, —I map the full journey, from content creation to user behaviour, and identify where processes break down. This allows me to spot issues early and design solutions that work at scale.

I focus on root cause, not symptoms. Where others react to surface-level problems, I trace issues back to their origin, whether that’s inconsistent content structures, fragmented journeys, or inefficient workflows. This approach consistently leads to more effective, long-term solutions.

My work has delivered measurable impact, including increasing CSAT from 66% to 78%, reducing translation spend from £925k to £125k through standardisation, and lowering contact centre demand by redesigning support journeys and enabling effective self-service.

I’m both analytical and pragmatic. I use data to guide decisions, but I’m equally comfortable designing solutions from first principles when needed. I’m known for delivering high-quality work and for being someone teams rely on to bring clarity to complex problems.

I’m now looking to apply this experience to work that has a more direct, meaningful impact on people’s lives—where improving user experience also delivers tangible social or human value.

I strongly believe that the best UX decisions aren’t always driven by immediate ROI. Long-term customer trust, usability, and reliability are often undervalued, but they are critical to retention, brand strength, and sustainable success.
